# Source Priority

Prefer sources in this order:

1. Official paper pages or publisher pages
2. arXiv pages / PDF landing pages
3. Official project pages / benchmark pages
4. Author pages or lab pages
5. Secondary summaries only when primary sources are unavailable

Rules:
- Do not cite blog summaries as if they were papers
- If metadata conflicts across sources, prefer the paper page or publisher record
- If the paper cannot be verified, mark it as tentative
