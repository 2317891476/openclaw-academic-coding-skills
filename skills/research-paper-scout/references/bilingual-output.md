# Bilingual Output

When the user wants Chinese output for research results, keep both layers:

## Layer 1: English-preserving record
Keep the original English title and source link. Preserve key benchmark names, method names, and proper nouns in English.

## Layer 2: Academic Chinese rendering
Add a Chinese rendering that is concise, formal, and publication-friendly.

Recommended card shape:

- Follow `references/default-bilingual-template.md` as the default structure.

Rules:
- Preserve model names, benchmark names, and dataset names in English when needed.
- Prefer academic Chinese wording over literal translation.
- Do not over-translate standard technical terms if the English term is clearer.
