## compare-bilingual
1. Original English
2. Polished English
3. Chinese academic rendering

Rules:
- Chinese rendering should preserve the scientific meaning of the English source.
- It should read like publishable academic Chinese, not literal machine translation.
- Keep citations, benchmark names, model names, and standard technical terms unchanged when appropriate.
