# Screening Rubric

Rank candidate papers by these factors:

## relevance
How directly it matches the user's topic.

## novelty
How useful it is for understanding the current frontier.

## authority
Prefer primary papers, benchmark pages, or strong venues.

## utility
How helpful the paper is for later writing, comparison, or implementation.

Use a practical mix:
- 60% highly relevant recent work
- 20% foundational papers
- 20% adjacent or contrastive work
