# Section Modes

## abstract
Compress aggressively; foreground problem, method, and key evidence.

## introduction
Improve motivation, problem framing, and contribution clarity.

## related_work
Strengthen comparison and transitions; remove list-like writing.

## method
Prefer precision and stable terminology over flourish.

## experiment
Make setup, metrics, and comparison language crisp.

## discussion
Clarify interpretation, limitations, and implications.

## rebuttal
Use calm, direct, evidence-based responses. Avoid defensive tone.
